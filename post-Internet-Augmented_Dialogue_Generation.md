## Internet-Augmented Dialogue Generation

Mojtaba Komeili, Kurt Shuster, Jason Weston; arxiv 2021

[[link]](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2107.07566.pdf)

**Summary**

Existing language models in chatbots sometimes cause 'Fact Hallucination' symptoms. This is a problem that arises from the fact that once a pretrained huge language model is learned from the point of view of language generation, there are times when misconceptions are stored, and the learning of new concepts that need to be updated occasionally is not being learned for a long time. To solve this problem, open-domain qa or augmentation of responses with a knowledge graph have been solved by borrowing external knowledge.

This paper is a paper that considers the Internet as external knowledge and augments the response through real-time search so that social chatbot can aggregate more general knowledge and reflect it in the Dialogue system.
